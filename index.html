About

Emerging in 2023 from Delhi, India, SØRB blends trap, dance pop, edm, house, techno, chillwave, rnb and neo soul into a genre-defying sound.

SØRB is a dynamic and genre-fluid DJ with over 2 years of experience delivering electrifying sets that seamlessly blend global and local sounds. Drawing inspiration from artists like Fred Again.., Martin Garrix, Avicii, Hardwell, Keinemusik, and Skrillex, he crafts immersive journeys that captivate audiences and keep the energy high.

Specializing in House, Melodic House, Bollywood, EDM, Deep Bass, Techno, and Trap, SØRB's performances are known for their versatility and ability to adapt to any crowd. His sets often transition between genres effortlessly, creating a unique sonic experience with every performance.

He has performed at various venues across the city and is currently the resident DJ at Valhalla Lounge, Indirapuram, where he curates the weekend vibe and keeps the dance floor moving. SØRB's focus is not just on playing music but on building moments — giving every audience a night to remember.
